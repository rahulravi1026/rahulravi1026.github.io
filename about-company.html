About Us

We are FTC Team 5773, a group of 11 students from American High School in Fremont, California, that strives to make the world better with robotics.

Our Mission
Innovate. Implement. Inspire.

Our mission is to connect with and inspire our local community by building STEM skills, encouraging a strong work ethic, and promoting leadership, self-confidence, and teamwork, in order to build our next generation.

Our team motto: Everyone can make a difference, but it has to be different!
